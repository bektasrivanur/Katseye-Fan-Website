WHO IS KATSEYE?

Katseye (pronounced "cat's eye") is a girl group based in Los Angeles. The group is composed of six members: Daniela Avanzini, Lara Raj, Manon Bannerman, Megan Skiendiel, Sophia Laforteza, and Yoonchae Jeung. With members from the Philippines, South Korea, Switzerland, and the United States, Katseye is often described as a "global girl group".

Group based in Los Angeles, formed through HYBE and Geffen Records’ 2023 project Dream Academy. Consisting of six members from four different countries, the group represents a new generation of multicultural pop artists. Since their debut in 2024, KATSEYE has gained international recognition with chart-topping releases and Grammy Award nominations.
