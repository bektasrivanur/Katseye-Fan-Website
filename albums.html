Soft Is Strong

SIS (Soft Is Strong) is KATSEYE’s first extended play, released in 2024. The album explores the idea that vulnerability and softness can coexist with confidence and strength. Through a mix of pop and emotional tracks, KATSEYE presents a fresh and empowering image. Soft Is Strong introduces the group’s identity and artistic direction to a global audience.

Beautiful Chaos

Beautiful Chaos is KATSEYE’s second extended play, released in 2025. The album embraces contrast by blending bold, experimental sounds with emotional depth. It represents growth, confidence, and the beauty found in imperfection. With this release, KATSEYE expands their musical identity and solidifies their presence on the global stage.
